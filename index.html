- Our Mission -

Needless to say, physical and mental health is fundamental for our differing lifestyles. Our website intends to simplify the accessibility between people and their medical history. If suddenly prompted, where would you search for yours? Maybe in a folder or filing cabinet stuffed with other cramped paperwork, or even “neatly organized”? Perhaps you would call your doctor(s) and ask them? Or maybe you would simply remember off the top of your head? Despite these approaches, virtually none of them would be as fast and accurate as simply looking it up in your own, secure account with Medivance.
